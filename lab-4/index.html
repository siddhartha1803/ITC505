Addendum: My Styling Choices

I wanted to create the layout in a professional manner without using heavy colors and other decorative effects.

  • Typography: I applied Inter to headings and Merriweather to the main text. Such a combination divides the titles of sections and the main part and maintains the flow of reading the whole text as effortless and current.
  • Colors: The off-white background with dark gray text is rather well-contrasting, and indigo accents create a slight and tech-like appearance that is both noticeable but still professional.
  • Layout: I have put the main content to the left-hand side and placed the Skills to the right in a sidebar. This makes the important details visible and the design appear balanced and more similar to a real resume design.
  • Spacing: To make the text open and easy to read, I used regular white spacing between paragraphs and list items. It also assists in making every section to shine out.
  • Details: The rounded skill labels, footer date and skip link give it slight usability and the polish without making the design too complex.
